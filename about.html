Our story from April 2016 to today

  • Founded April 2016
    In April 2016, our journey began with a humble start in a shared office space, where a small team shared a vision for something greater. Born out of a collective passion for innovation, our company embarked on a mission to build something extraordinary. In those shared office walls, ideas flourished, collaborations blossomed, and the foundation was laid for the exceptional brand we've become today.
  • Our first customer
    Our inaugural customer, a visionary entrepreneur, took a chance on us in the early days of our journey, becoming the catalyst for our growth. Their trust and partnership fueled our determination to deliver unparalleled service. Their success became our success, setting the tone for the lasting relationships we continue to build with each valued client.
  • First major project
    Classgist was our first major project and it marked a significant milestone in our company's evolution. Undertaken with enthusiasm and a commitment to excellence, this endeavor pushed the boundaries of innovation and showcased our capabilities. Successfully navigating the complexities of the project not only solidified our expertise but also laid the groundwork for the ambitious ventures that followed.
  • Hiring and outsourcing strategy
    Our hiring and outsourcing strategy is meticulously designed to assemble a dynamic and skilled team that aligns with our company's vision and values. We prioritize attracting top-tier talent, fostering a collaborative work environment, and providing continuous learning opportunities to ensure our team stays at the forefront of industry trends. Additionally, we strategically leverage outsourcing partnerships to augment our capabilities, tapping into specialized expertise and resources that enhance our overall efficiency and innovation. This dual approach enables us to maintain a nimble and adept workforce, ready to tackle diverse challenges and drive sustained success.
  • Current activities - Crafting Cutting-Edge Solutions
    We are at the forefront of technological advancements, leveraging the latest tools and frameworks to develop solutions that redefine industry standards. Our commitment to innovation is embedded in our development process, ensuring that every project benefits from state-of-the-art technologies, fostering efficiency, scalability, and unparalleled performance. From embracing cloud-native architectures to harnessing the power of machine learning and artificial intelligence, our development teams are adept at integrating the latest technological trends into our solutions. This strategic approach not only future-proofs our products but also empowers our clients with transformative, forward-looking solutions that stand out in today's dynamic digital landscape. By consistently staying abreast of emerging technologies, we ensure that our clients not only meet their current objectives but also position themselves for success in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
  • Today
    Our vision is to evolve as a trailblazer in the industry, pioneering groundbreaking solutions that anticipate and meet the ever-changing needs of our clients. Looking ahead, we are committed to expanding our global footprint, fostering strategic partnerships, and enhancing our service portfolio to cater to emerging market demands. Embracing sustainability, inclusivity, and cutting-edge technology will remain pivotal in our endeavors, as we aspire to contribute meaningfully to both local and global communities.
