Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to enhance the user experience, provide website analytics, and store information about user preferences

How We Use Cookies

  1. Improve Website Functionality:

    Cookies enable the proper functioning of our website, allowing you to navigate smoothly and access essential features.

  2. Enhance User Experience:

    We utilize cookies to customize your experience, remembering your preferences and settings for a more personalized visit.

  3. Analyze Website Traffic:

    Cookies help us gather information about how visitors interact with our website. This data is valuable for analyzing and improving our content and user interface.

  4. Marketing and Advertising:

    We may use cookies for targeted advertising purposes, helping us deliver relevant content and promotions based on your interests.

Types of Cookies We Use

  1. Essential Cookies:

    Necessary for the basic functionality of our website, these cookies enable core features like page navigation and access to secure areas.

  2. Performance Cookies:

    These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, helping us understand and improve its performance.

  3. Functionality Cookies:

    Functionality cookies allow us to remember your preferences, providing a more personalized and efficient experience..

  4. Targeting and Advertising Cookies:

    Used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests, these cookies help us tailor our marketing efforts to suit your needs.

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. However, disabling cookies may impact your ability to fully utilize our website.

Changes to Our Cookies Policy

We may update our Cookies Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
